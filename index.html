About The Client

Created by the Iconic Bar Company, Somerset House’s warm hospitality and delicious food won Star Pubs & Bars 'Best Food Pub of the Year 2014'. With seasonally changing menus based on locally sourced produce, as well as the very best in service and eclectic surroundings, it is a great place to go for a laid back breakfast with the whole family, quiet business lunch or a sumptuous dinner.

The Challenge

To create a responsive website that that shows of what a great venue The Somerset House is and to help them stand out from the competition in the Clifton area. The site needed to visually capture peoples attention and give them a feel for what the pub is all about. Key information needed to be easily accessible and easily manageable by the guys at The Somerset House, so they could keep the site up to date themselves.
